SoC Diploma Courses

Diploma in Common Information Technology Programme
(S32)


A semester long programme aimed to provide students with a
broad introduction to the Infocomm Landscape &
insights to the various different career paths in the IT industry.

Allows students to make informed decision
when selecting one out of the three SOC diploma courses to pursue:
DIT, DISM or DAAA.

2022 Planned Intake: 165

Range of Net 2022 JAE ELR2B2: 3 to 13

Diploma in Infocomm Technology
(S69)

Focuses on Software & Applications development with 3 specialist tracks:
Software Development, User Experience (UX) & Immersive Simulation to improve user experience.

Future Careers:
UX Designer, UI Designer, Software Engineer, etc.

2022 Planned Intake: 160

Range of Net 2022 JAE ELR2B2: 4 to 14

Diploma in Infocomm Security Management
(S54)

With rise of cycber attakcs, this diploma aims to cover the areas of security technology, security management, information technology, law & teamwork & communications to prepare students entering the cycber industry.

Future Careers:
Forensic Investigator, Security Engineer, Cyber Risk Analyst, etc.

2022 Planned Intake: 120

Range of Net 2022 JAE ELR2B2: 4 to 11

Diploma in Appiled AI & Analytics
(S30)

Aims to teach students to apply AI & big data to solve current solutions across industries, such as finance, information technology & commerce to support the rising demand in AI-related roles & skills.
